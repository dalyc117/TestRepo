Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my profile information?

Go to your profile page and click the "Edit Profile" button. You can update your information, profile picture, and preferences there.

How do I change my password?

Visit the Settings page and select the "Security" tab. You'll find the option to change your password there.

How do I log a workout?

Click on "Log Workout" in the navigation bar and fill out the workout details form. You can specify the type, duration, and intensity of your workout.

Can I edit or delete a logged workout?

Yes, you can edit or delete any workout from your workout history. Go to your profile and find the workout in your activity log.

How do challenges work?

Challenges are time-based fitness goals that you can join. Complete the required activities within the time limit to earn points and rewards.

How are achievements earned?

Achievements are unlocked by reaching specific milestones in your fitness journey, such as completing a certain number of workouts or maintaining a streak.
